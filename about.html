Who are We?
Repaints Plus is a small Flight Sim based Company, that makes
Scenery and Liveries for Microsoft Flight Simulator 2020, and
X-Plane 11, looking to expand to Lockheed Martin's Prepar3d in
the future. We pride ourselves on Quality over Quantity. At any
time, we are working on at least one Livery, and One Scenery.
This helps us remain somewhat relevant. Our Liveries and
Sceneries are made with extreme care, and all created by hand.
